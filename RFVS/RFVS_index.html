Our Mission

The Keystone Re:Foundry Venture Studio was formed to solve a critical and growing problem facing Pennsylvaniaâ€™s industrial communities. Across the region, many strong and profitable small and mid sized manufacturers anchor their communities and local supply chains, yet they face the risk of disappearing because there is no clear path for ownership succession or continued operation. When even just one of these companies closes, entire networks feel the impact of this loss. Good jobs vanish, critical knowledge is lost, and the industrial foundation that built American and still supports local communities becomes weaker. But we do not believe this decline and loss of businesses to be inevitable. It is a structural gap that has gone unaddressed for too long.

The Keystone Re:Foundry is a venture studio that will fill this gap by stepping in as a committed partner. We will identify companies across the region with the potential for renewed success and we will stabilize their operations, modernize their systems, unlock new growth opportunities, prepare the next generation of owner operators, and set each firm on a path that supports ongoing growth and an a permanent presence in their communities. Our mission is simple. Preserve the companies that hold our communities together. Build a stronger, more resilient industrial base. Prove that with the right structure and the right vision, local industry can thrive. Our success will contribute to the reindustrialization of America, but its impact runs deeper. It will restore confidence, ignite momentum, and help shape a bright future worth cheering for in the communities we love and call home.
