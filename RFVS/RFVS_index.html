Preserving Northwest Pennsylvania's Manufacturing Legacy

Our Mission

Thousands of small manufacturing firms across Northwest Pennsylvania face a succession crisis as founders who built these businesses in the 1980s and 1990s approach retirement without successors. These companies are profitable and essential to regional supply chains, but they fall into a market gapâ€”too small for private equity, too operationally informal for traditional lending, and too capital-intensive for individual buyers. Each closure eliminates skilled jobs, disrupts supply chains, and erodes the industrial knowledge base that sustains the region's economy.

Refounder Studio represents a comprehensive solution to an existential threat facing Northwest Pennsylvania's industrial economy. By preventing closures, we preserve productive capacity, protect employment, maintain supply chain integrity, transfer technical knowledge across generations, and demonstrate that manufacturing businesses can thrive under new ownership. The alternative is the continued erosion of the region's industrial foundation, with consequences that compound across employment, community stability, and economic resilience.
