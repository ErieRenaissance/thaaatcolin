Our Mission

The Keystone Re:Foundry is a 501(c)3 nonprofit venture studio that was formed to solve a critical and growing problem facing Pennsylvania's industrial communities.

There are profitable small and mid-sized manufacturers that anchor communities and local supply chains across the Commonwealth, yet they risk disappearing because they lack a clear path for ownership succession or continued operation. When even one of these companies closes, entire communities feel the effects of the loss. Good jobs vanish, knowledge is lost, and the industrial foundation that built American becomes weaker.

We do not believe the decline and loss of these businesses is inevitable, but that it is a structural gap that has gone unaddressed for too long.

The Keystone Re:Foundry will fill this gap and prevent the loss of these businesses as a temporary steward that implements operational improvements and facilitates the transition to the next-generation of leaders. By focusing on companies with the potential for renewed success, providing them with the support, resources, and expertise needed to modernize operations, pursue growth and new market opportunities, and position the next generation of owner-operators to achieve long-term success that secures a permanent presence in their communities.

Our mission is simple. Save the companies that hold our communities together, build a stronger, more resilient industrial base, and demonstrate that local industry can thrive with the right support and right vision. Our success contributes to American reindustrialization, but the impact extends far beyond economics. We are working to restore confidence, spark new enthusiasm, and secure a future we can all be proud of in the communities we call home.
